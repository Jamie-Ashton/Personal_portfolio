About Me

busy doing something...

Hi, I’m jamie I’m originally from Ashburton but my family move up to nelson mid around 1997 when our home burnt down most of my wider family are still stuck down there trying to escape…

I attended the normal schools in stoke Nayland kindergarten, Nayland primary, broad green (I don’t know why they didn’t call it Nayland intermediate) then on to Nayland college. College was a blast I only really went to eat my lunch and see my mates but I injoyed automotive engineering and through it was worth pursuing.

When I left college, I didn’t want to study so I choose to study at NTI (it was all free back in 2014) and at that stage I didn’t really know what I was doing and didn’t want a student loan hanging over my head.

When I finished my pre trade course I got stuck into it and landed my first real job at Phil Nichols Auto Centre which is still operating in Richmond (next to BK). I didn’t last long, there wasn’t enough work coming through and I spent most of the time cleaning.

Then I landed an apprenticeship through MS Motors starting at their Richmond service centre, then moving to the main dealership in town where I worked for 5 years getting different qualifications in the brands we worked with these where Ford, Kia and Can am. The highest I got was a Ford Ranger Specialist which I got from a training course in Melbourne.

Then I moved across the road to Haven Motors to try sales where I worked for about 3 years getting some good business knowledge and fundamentals working with local panel beaters, workshops and cash customers. I didn’t particularly like the job some customers were challenging and it was a constant balancing act.

Currently studying IT at NMIT pursuing a bachelor’s degree and managing and working a Crest Clean Franchise so I can pay the mortgage and have some pocket money.
